Description:

ThermoLine Mask is specially formulated with resins, natural oils and extracts to provide maximum hydration and softness to the hair. It has anti-drying action on the hair, prolonging the effect of the ironing and giving radiance shine to the hair.


Directions for Use:

Wash hair with the shampoo and rinse it to completely remove the shampoo. Apply on clean, wet hair, spreading evenly throughout hair. Leave it on for 5 to 10 minutes. Rinse it and finish
it as desired.
