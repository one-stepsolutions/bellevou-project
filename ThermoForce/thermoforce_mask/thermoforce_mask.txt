Description:

ThermoForce Mask contains active complex which helps to infuse essential nutrients to the hair to start internal restoration of the hair damaged by chemical processes and external
aggressions giving hair softness and intense shine. Coats the hair fibers with an intense anti-frizz action and conditioning, giving strength and resistance to the hair.


Directions for Use:

Wash hair with the shampoo and rinse it to completely remove the shampoo. Apply on clean, wet hair, spreading evenly throughout hair. Leave it on for 5 to 10 minutes. Rinse it and finish
it as desired.
