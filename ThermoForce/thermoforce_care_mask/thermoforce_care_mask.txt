Description:

ThermoForce Care Mask developed with protein forming acids deeply nourishes, rebuilds hair fiber. Enriched with Phospholipids, Glycolipids and Phytosterols, it forms film around the hair structure, offers protection and improves moisture balance of the hair, protects the scalp skin from repeated washing.


Directions for Use:

Apply a small amount on clean and moist hair, massaging gently over the entire hair. Let it sit for 5 to 10 minutes. Rinse out the extra care product using lukewarm water until the hair is squeaky clean.. Finalize as you wish.
